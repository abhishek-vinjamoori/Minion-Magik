After running the makefile run the executable "sample2D".

The controls of the game are as follows - 

Player controls ~

UP Arrow      - Player moves forward
Down Arrow    - Player moves backward
Left Arrow    - Player moves towards left
Rigth Arrow   - Player moves towards right
Space Bar     - Player Jumps



Camera Views ~

(Normal Numbers on the keyboard are mentioned)

1 - Tower view.
2 - Top follow view.
3 - Fixed Top View
4 - Players First person View
5 - A Camera which is just behind the player and follows it.
6 - A Camera which is comfortable for playing the game
7 - Helicopter View.
Left mouse Press and move - Panning for X and Y axes.
Right mouse Press and move - Panning for Z axis.

Scroll - Zoom in and Zoom out
